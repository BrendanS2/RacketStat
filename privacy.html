Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

RacketStat ("we", "us", or "our") is currently in early development. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when joining our waitlist at racket-stat.vercel.app.

What we collect

We collect only your email address when you sign up for our waitlist. We do not collect your name, location, payment information, or any other personal data at this stage.

How we use it

  • To notify you when RacketStat launches
  • To send you a link to create your free account at launch
  • We will not send you marketing emails, spam, or sell your email to anyone

Data storage

Your email is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party database provider. Supabase stores data in the United States. You can read Supabase's privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy.

Your rights

You can request deletion of your email at any time by contacting us. Simply email us and we will remove your data within 7 days.

Analytics

We use Vercel Analytics to understand how many people visit our site. This is privacy-friendly, cookie-free analytics — no personal data is collected or stored.
